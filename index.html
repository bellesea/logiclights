LogicLights ✨

How to use:
  • Right click to add elements (switches or gates)
  • A switch is an input (tick the box for the input to be true)
  • You can pick from AND, OR, NAND, and NOR gates
  • Each gate requires two inputs, which can come from switches or another gate or a combination
  • To connect two elements, click and drag your cursor from the green circle on your first element to the green circle on your second element
  • The true/false text in the gate is the output
  • You can move elements around by clicking and dragging the rectangles
  • You can zoom in and out to create more room for elements
